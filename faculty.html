Our FACULTY ADVISOR

MD Shiyan Sadik

Full-Time Faculty, Lecturer at North South University

M.Sc. in Economic Development, Adam Smith Business School, University of Glasgow

B.S.S. in Economics, University of Dhaka

MD Shiyan Sadik is a highly accomplished academic and professional, dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders at North South University. With a strong background in economics and extensive experience in diverse professional roles, he brings a wealth of knowledge and insight to the Earth Club. As a former Research Associate at the Centre for Policy Dialogue (CPD), an Assistant Operations Executive at Marks and Spencer, and an Onboarding Executive at Foodpanda, he has honed his expertise in research, management, and organizational development.

Beyond his academic and professional endeavors, MD Shiyan Sadik is also an active debater, presenter, and researcher. He is a proud alumnus of the University of Glasgow and the University of Dhaka, as well as St. Joseph Higher Secondary School. His passion for education, economics, and mentorship makes him an invaluable asset to the Earth Club and its initiatives.
